Band Members

Alex Turner

Role: Lead Vocals / Rhythm Guitar

Alex Turner is the dynamic frontman of The Black Horizon, known for his powerful vocals and rhythmic guitar skills. His stage presence and emotional delivery captivate audiences worldwide.

Jade Ramirez

Role: Lead Guitar / Backing Vocals

Jade Ramirez's intricate guitar solos and harmonies add depth to the band's sound. Her technical prowess and creative flair are a driving force behind The Black Horizon's music.

Dylan Evans

Role: Bass Guitar

Dylan Evans lays down the foundation with his solid bass lines. His groove and rhythm are essential to the band's progressive and post-rock fusion.

Liam Patel

Role: Drums / Percussion

Liam Patel's complex drumming patterns and energetic performance keep the band in perfect sync. His versatility behind the drum kit adds dynamic layers to their live shows.

Kendra Lee

Role: Keyboards / Synths

Kendra Lee's atmospheric keyboards and synths provide the band's signature cosmic soundscapes. Her contributions enrich the band's melodic and ambient elements.
